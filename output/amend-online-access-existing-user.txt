
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Amend online access for an existing user 
  
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Complete a Mandate Variation form to update who can access Online for Business, what type of access they have and, for delegates, what accounts they can see. 
 Here are some of the changes you can make 
 
 Upgrade to Full Access User status. 
 Change to Delegate User status. 
 De-register an Online for Business user. 
 Update who can access your business accounts, change your mailing address and amend your signing instructions. 
 
 To register a new user, you can either request this within  Online for Business   or complete our  online registration form .
