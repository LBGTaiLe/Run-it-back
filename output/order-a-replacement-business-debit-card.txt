






Order a replacement Business Debit Card


Order a replacement Business Debit Card if your current one is damaged. 
If your card is lost or stolen, it’s important you report this to us immediately.
























I use Online for Business




Log in to Online for Business.
Go to More actions next to the account you need the card for.
Select Account services then Request replacement card.
Complete the on-screen instructions and select Submit.

You’ll receive your replacement card in 5-7 working days.




























Chat in the mobile app



In the business banking app, go to Support then Message us.
Ask the assistant to ‘Order a replacement debit card’.
You’ll receive your replacement card in 5-7 working days.
